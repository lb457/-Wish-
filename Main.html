28 March 2024 — The Day Everything Changed

🚌
28 March 2024 · A Random Day
The Bus, The Stop, The Eyes

We were both coming back from our hometowns — strangers on the same road. At Stop 70, our eyes met. Just a glance. But it was enough to make the world pause for a second.

🪑
A Few Minutes Later
You Sat Next to Me

You got up from your seat and sat beside me. The moment you were next to me, I felt this calm, warm energy — something I had never felt before. Like the universe had quietly arranged this.

💬
2–3 Hours of Pure Magic
We Talked... And Forgot the World

For 2–3 hours we talked non-stop — and we were so lost in conversation that we forgot to even ask each other's names. Time was passing fast, but I didn't want it to. As Jaipur got closer, something inside me got heavier.

📱
The Last Moment
You Asked for My Chat ID

At the very end, that's when you asked for my chat ID — and that's when I finally learned your name. But I didn't like it when you said "Bhai" at the end. You were already someone I knew I wanted to know forever.

🌸
Every Day After That
Your Words Stayed With Me

Every day we talked, that day became special. I remember every conversation, every small thing you shared — your simplicity, your nature, your smile. I wrote them all in my mind. I still have them.

💕
29 March 2026 · 2 Years Later
You Are Still My Special One

Two years have passed since that day. Things changed, conversations paused — but what I felt never changed. Not even for a single day. You have always been, and will always be, my most special person. 💕

Jo Keh Na Saka, Yahan Likha Hai

Wo bus ki khidki, wo stop number 70,
wo teri pehli nazar — sab yaad hai mujhe.
Naam bhi na poochha tha ghanton baat karke,
par dil ne jaana tha — yeh koi khaas hai.

— 28 March 2024 ✦

Teri choti choti baaton ko dil mein likha hai,
teri smile ka koi jawab nahi mere paas.
Baat ho ya na ho, tum special rahi ho hamesha —
yeh sirf main jaanta hoon, aur ab tum bhi jaan lo.

— always, from the heart ✦

Sneha, main jaanta hoon bahut waqt beet gaya. Baatein band hui, dooriyan aayi — par jo feel tha wo kabhi nahi gaya. Har din tumse baat karne ka dil kiya, par na kar paya — pata nahi kyun. Aaj bhi nahi pata.

Main tumse ek mauka maangna chahta hoon. Sirf ek.
Kya tum mujhe jaan ne dogi?

Tumhari pasand ke saath chalenge — bahar khana, favourite jagah jaana, garam chai peena saath mein jab thandi ho... jo tumhe pasand ho. Agar tumhari life mein koi aur hai, to main bilkul samjhunga. Par agar na ho — to please ek baar sochna.

Tum mere liye hamesha special rahi ho. Happy birthday, Sneha. God tumhe woh sab de jo tum deserve karti ho — aur tum bahut kuch deserve karti ho. 💕
